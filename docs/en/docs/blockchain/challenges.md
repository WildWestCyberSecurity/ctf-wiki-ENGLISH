## Blockchain Security Challenges in CTF

!!! note 
    Note: Blockchain challenges in CTF competitions are not very common, so we have collected the majority of available challenges, including smart contract and public blockchain related challenges. They are listed by year with links to the challenges and their writeups. The challenge list is incomplete and will be updated regularly. Competition names within the same year are not listed in any particular order, and if there are multiple writeups, they are also not in any particular order. Related challenges can be found in the [ctf-challenges/blockchain](https://github.com/ctf-wiki/ctf-challenges/tree/master/blockchain) repository or practiced online at the [ChainFlag](https://chainflag.org/) platform.
